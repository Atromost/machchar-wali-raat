# 🦟 Machchar Wali Raat!!

## How to Play

Move your cursor to chase mosquitoes across the screen and click to squash them. Each hit gives you points and plays a satisfying **PUCHHAK** sound.

* Click fast to kill normal mosquitoes.
* Watch for fast red mosquitoes — they move quicker.
* Catch the rare **Golden Mosquito** for bonus points.
* Don't let mosquitoes escape off-screen.

## Features

* Mouse-based reflex gameplay.
* Satisfying hit sound effects.
* Golden Mosquito bonus scoring.
* Escaping mosquitoes cost you points.
* Fast-paced rounds.
